-PORTUGUES
INSTRUCOES (PORTUGUES)

Jogando -> Pasta Builds, pasta BFs, Windows_x86.zip

Barra de espaco -> arma especial (pode ser pega ao matar o primeiro boss, apesar de nao ser possivel ve-la ela estara la em baixo de onde ele estava [eh necessario pegar sua arma de novo apos isso])

E -> pegar arma do chao (trocar com a da mao)

W A S D -> movimentar personagem

Esc -> abrir "menu"/hud detro do jogo (nao pausa o jogo)

Mouse -> mirar
Mouse (botao esquerdo) -> atirar 
Mouse (botao direito) -> tiro especial

-ENGLISH
INSTRUCTIONS (ENGLISH)

Play -> Builds folder, BFs folder, Windows_x86.zip

Space bar -> special weapon (can be found after killing the first boss, although it is not possible to see it, the weapon will be on the same spot where the boss was [you will need to get your gun again after this])

E -> pick up gun from the ground (switch with hand)

W A S D -> move character

Esc -> open "menu"/hud while playing (do not pause the game)

Mouse -> aim
Mouse (left button) -> Shoot
Mouse (right button) -> Special shoot
